Welcome to Empowering the Nation

Empowering the Nation is an initiative by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. The training helps them upskill, gain confidence, and become more marketable in their work or even start their own businesses.

We offer six-month and six-week training programmes in areas like First Aid, Sewing, Landscaping, Life Skills, Child Minding, Cooking, and Garden Maintenance.
